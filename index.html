About Photography
Photography is the art of capturing light with a camera, usually via a digital sensor or film,
to create an image. The word “photography” comes from the Greek words “photos”
meaning “light” and “graphos” meaning “drawing” or “writing.” It is a form of visual
communication that has become an integral part of our daily lives.
